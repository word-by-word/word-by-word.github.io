Speech-to-text

Have difficulty with English pronunciation? Word by Word robust speech-to-text capabilities let you recognize spoken words via text, making learning and practicing much easier and intuitive.

Personalized Learning

Our speech recognition software performs assessment of preparedness for exams, proficiency in speaking and reading and international communication for English language learners helping them to pinpoint the exact syllables that a user is speaking incorrectly regardless of your accent and prints what was intended.

Instant Feedback

Immediate feedback shows what was said and compares it to the way it should have been spoken filling in the correct word(s) you may have missed.

Performance Measurement

Returns percentage of correctly pronounced words as performance metrics.
